About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) has nineteen faculty with doctoral degrees, and nine are pursuing doctoral studies. Faculty members have been publishing in refereed journals and conference proceedings. The department conducts vocational and proficiency courses on fundamental and new programming languages and computer science concepts, beyond college hours or during summer semesters. Some faculty are involved in institutional-level activities and interdisciplinary research. State-of-the-art laboratories like SAP, IBM Centre of Excellence, and Cuda learning center are available. Technical seminars, workshops, and hackathons are conducted regularly for UG & PG students. The department encourages students to participate in extracurricular/sports activities and offers an active alumni network. The department is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for a period of 3 years and has MoUs with leading IT industries like NVIDIA, SAP, IBM, and HP. Subjects with hands-on sessions are emphasized, and students are encouraged to take up MOOC-based online courses on platforms like NPTEL, IITBombayX, Coursera, Udacity, and edX.
